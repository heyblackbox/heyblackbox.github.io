How we approached design

Before starting, we agreed that the app should keep to the following principles:

  • Focus on music, not "entertainment": this was our mantra at Zvooq. We treat music very seriously, and considered our mission to bring back its value as a work of art.
  • Subscription price must be as low as possible. Users in Russia’s highly pirated market were not ready to pay much for music. Our second goal was to grow this idea that it's not nonsense to pay a little bit for something you love.
  • The app must be a part of the existing ecosystem of Zvooq service. Increase the value of the service to the user (on both web and mobile platforms).
