Fonoteka

Mobile app that brings back the value of a music record.

The challenge

To design an app that will deliver offline music experience and will have the lowest subscription price possible.

We wanted to plant the idea of paying something for music access. On the Russian market users are used to getting music for free. So we needed to come up with something more convenient than free options, and so cheap that it will be “almost free”.

VIDEO

Design

Reflection on the ways people interact with mobile music, I understood that the limitation that allows for a cheap subscription can also be a good experience. And it can be turned into app marketing proposition.

Be closer to the music. Bring back the value of a record.

The app offers a limited amount of offline music, while still providing access to whole user collection and a catalog of a mothership service.
