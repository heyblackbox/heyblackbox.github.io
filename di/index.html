DI

Visualizing company culture in a collective image blog.


An image of a digital product company is defined by its products, people, and culture. I think this website makes a balanced presentation of these three key parts, without unnecessary noise. That's why I put it here.

The website helps job seekers, journalists and anyone interested to look at what's going on inside Dream Industries. What they see is a democratic visual feed. To post there, employees can add a special tag to pictures they share to Instagram. The website scrapes those tagged images, and if an author is a current DI employee, the picture is added to the feed. The image feed counterweighs the formal parts such as job openings and mission statement.

This design works both for people both inside and outside of the company. It gives employees an easy way to add to the public image of the company, feel accomplished at work. For non-employees, it provides a view on what's inside, communicates democratic values and open culture of DI.

DI is a digital product company developing content services for digital books and music, as well as operating a coworking and event space in the center of Moscow.
